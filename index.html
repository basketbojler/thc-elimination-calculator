About This Calculator

Goodwin RS, Darwin WD, Chiang CN, Shih M, Li SH, Huestis MA.

"Urinary Elimination of 11-Nor-9-carboxy-9-tetrahydrocannabinol in Cannabis Users During Continuously Monitored Abstinence"

J Anal Toxicol. 2008;32(8):562-569 — NIDA, National Institutes of Health

Read the full study on PubMed →

Estimates are based on 60 monitored participants and 6,158 urine specimens collected over up to 30 days of confirmed abstinence. Your usage, amount, duration, and BMI are used to classify you into one of three research groups to predict elimination timelines.

Results are population-level estimates. Individual results vary based on metabolism, hydration, diet, and exercise. This is not medical or legal advice.
